Key Lime Pie

Description

You have never tasted anything so delicious and easy to make

Ingredients

  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 limes
  • graham crackers
  • butter

Steps

  1. mash the graham crackers and butter together until mixed
  2. press mixture into a pie form
  3. zest limes and squeeze lime juice. Combine.
  4. stir in sweetened condensed milk
  5. pour mixture into pie crust
  6. refrigerate for 4 hours
  7. serve with whipped cream and lime zest
